## Vision

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly evolving from chatbots into autonomous agents capable of making decisions, interacting with humans, and performing complex tasks.

As AI systems become more autonomous, ensuring their safety cannot stop at deployment. They require continuous monitoring, behavioral analysis, and trust evaluation throughout their operational lifecycle.

SentinelAI is a research-driven open-source initiative that aims to develop a continuous AI Safety Observatory for monitoring, evaluating, and improving the trustworthiness of autonomous AI systems.

---

## Mission

Develop an AI safety platform capable of

- Monitoring AI behavior continuously
- Detecting unsafe behavioral changes
- Measuring trustworthiness
- Evaluating reasoning consistency
- Identifying hallucination risks
- Providing explainable safety reports
