# SentinelAI Project Vision

## Vision

To contribute to trustworthy AI by exploring methods for continuously monitoring and evaluating AI behavior after deployment.

---

## Mission

SentinelAI is a research prototype that investigates how organizations can improve AI trustworthiness through continuous safety evaluation, explainable assessments, and human oversight.

---

## Problem

Most AI safety techniques focus on model training and pre-deployment evaluation.

As AI systems become increasingly autonomous, organizations also need methods to observe AI behavior during real-world operation.

---

## Proposed Solution

SentinelAI introduces a post-deployment AI safety layer that evaluates AI responses using the Sentinel Safety Framework (SSF).

The prototype combines:

- Risk Classification
- Safety Dimension Evaluation
- Sentinel Safety Score
- Explainable Recommendations
- Safety Dashboard

---

## Objectives

- Study current AI safety research.
- Design a structured safety evaluation framework.
- Build a working research prototype.
- Demonstrate continuous AI safety monitoring.
- Encourage further research into trustworthy AI systems.

---

## Expected Impact

SentinelAI aims to help researchers and developers think beyond one-time AI evaluations by exploring continuous monitoring approaches for deployed AI systems.

---

## Long-Term Roadmap

### Version 1.0
Research prototype and framework.

### Version 2.0
Interactive dashboard for AI safety evaluation.

### Version 3.0
Support for multiple AI models and benchmarking.

### Version 4.0
Behavior tracking and long-term safety analytics.

### Version 5.0
Open-source platform for AI safety research.

---

## Disclaimer

SentinelAI is an academic research prototype intended to explore ideas in AI safety. It is not a certified safety evaluation system and should not be used as the sole basis for high-stakes decisions.
